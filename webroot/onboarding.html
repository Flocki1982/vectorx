Vector Onboarding

To use your bots with VectorX and Wirepod, you need to onboard them, i.e. telling them to connect them to your RPi rather than using any server on the internet.

This step needs to be done only once per robot. The onboarding procedure differs whether your bot is a regular, production robot or it runs OSKR.

PRODUCTION ROBOTS

Open this link with Google Chrome:

https://keriganc.com/vector-epod-setup/html/main.html

and follow the instructions.
